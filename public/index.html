Methodology & Assumptions

Streaming rates are based on publicly reported per-stream averages: Spotify ~$0.004, Apple Music ~$0.008, YouTube Music ~$0.002, Amazon Music ~$0.004, Tidal ~$0.010. Actual rates vary by country, subscription tier, and label deals.

Cross-platform extrapolation assumes the artist has proportional presence on other platforms based on industry averages: Apple Music (~35% of Spotify streams), YouTube Music (~60%), Amazon Music (~20%), Tidal (~5%).

Audience Score is a composite of monthly listeners, follower ratio, genre multiplier, and estimated social reach. Scale: 0–100.

Merch potential is estimated using audience engagement, genre affinity for merchandise, and comparable artist benchmarks. Conversion rates range from 0.5–3% of active monthly listeners.

Catalog valuation uses a revenue multiple (typically 10–30x annual) based on growth trajectory, catalog depth, and audience demographics.

Connect Spotify API

  1. Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard
  2. Create a new app (any name, set redirect URI to http://localhost:3000)
  3. Copy your Client ID and Client Secret
  4. Create a .env file in the project root: 
    SPOTIFY_CLIENT_ID=your_id_here
SPOTIFY_CLIENT_SECRET=your_secret_here
  5. Restart the server

The calculator works in demo mode without credentials, but live data gives more accurate results.
